## Cable a tierra
En la tienda parece muy normal, pero guardo el link por si algo.
https://store.rakwireless.com/products/1-meter-lightning-arrestor-10-awg-grounding-wire
### Product Description
RAKwireless recommends a lightning arrestor to be installed on all the N-Type antenna terminals (LoRa, LTE, Wi-Fi and GPS).
It is recommended to use a 10 AWG(Coppet)/8 AWG (Aluminum)/17 AWG (Copper-Clad Steel) or better grounding wire to connect the arrestor to the tower mounted LoRa antenna
